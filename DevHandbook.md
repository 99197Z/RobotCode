# Debuging and Testing


## Error Codes
### 0x11XY - Motor OverHeat
the motor at XY is overheating

X: side of robot 
    - L - 0
    - R - 1
Y: motor pos
    - Forward - 0 
    - Aft     - 1 

### 0x10XY - data logging
#### 0x1001 - sd card missing
plug in the sd card

#### 0x1010 - Saved
Log Saved
