# Debuging and Testing

## Error Codes
### 0b11XY - Motor OverHeat
the motor at XY is overheating

X: side of robot 
    - L - 0
    - R - 1
Y: motor pos
    - Forward - 0 
    - Aft     - 1 

### 0b10XY - data logging
#### 0b1001 - sd card missing
plug in the sd card

#### 0b1010 - Saved
Log Saved

### 0b0111 - Competition Setup
use the brain's screen to pick atton
